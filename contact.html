Support
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I track my order?▼

Track your order in real-time from your account dashboard. Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email to monitor delivery status on our platform.

What is your return policy?▼

We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be in original condition with all packaging. Initiate a return from your account and we'll handle pickup and refund.

How does AI-powered inventory work?▼

Our AI engine uses XGBoost & regression to analyze sales patterns, seasonal trends, and market data — predicting demand with 99.2% accuracy to keep shelves optimally stocked.

What are the vendor subscription plans?▼

We offer Starter (₹999/month), Professional (₹4,999/month) with advanced analytics, and Enterprise (custom pricing) for large-scale retailers with dedicated support.

Is my payment information secure?▼

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and PCI DSS compliance. Your payment info is never stored on our servers — we partner with trusted payment gateways.

How do I become a vendor?▼

Visit the Vendor Portal, sign up, verify your business documents, and configure your inventory. Onboarding typically completes within 2–3 business days with our team's support.
